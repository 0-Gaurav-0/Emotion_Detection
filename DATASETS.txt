Datasets Used in This Project
=============================

Task 1 – Facial Emotion Detection
---------------------------------
- Dataset: FER-2013
- Link: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/deadskull7/fer2013
- Description: A widely used dataset containing 35,887 grayscale images of facial expressions across 7 emotion classes.

Task 2 – Animal Detection
-------------------------
- Dataset: Animals (YOLOv5)
- Link: https://universe.roboflow.com/yolo-2xmbu/animals-yolov5-gslnk/dataset/1
- Description: Custom object detection dataset for animal classification trained in YOLO format.

Task 3 – Audio Emotion Recognition
----------------------------------
- Dataset: RAVDESS (Ryerson Audio-Visual Database of Emotional Speech and Song)
- Link: https://zenodo.org/records/1188976
- Description: A high-quality dataset of 24 actors producing emotional speech and song recordings with 8 emotion classes.
